Alpha Lambda Mu

Our Mission is to establish a Muslim Network as well as a Brotherhood dedicated to not only improving ourselves but our communities. We hope to develop each and every brother into a community leader who will go back and help out their communities when they graduate and leave us. Above all, our mission is to better ourselves as People, for we are the future and if we ever want the future to be brighter, we must enlighten ourselves first.
